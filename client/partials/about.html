About this app

This soccer app is inspired by the popular game: FIFA 2016 by @EA Sports. You can select 18 players from packs to form your favorite team.

You can set up your customized formation and choose your best 11 on the pitch.

Player Data are crawled from @EA Sports Servers.

Players/nations/clubs images are from @Futhead community.

Tournament bracket generation script is developed based on @SterlingWes's excellent script.
